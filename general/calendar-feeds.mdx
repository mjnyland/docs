title: "Setting Up Calendar Feeds"
description: "Getting Started with Daysheets"
---

Daysheets allows you to calendar feeds to easily stream Daysheets details to you or your team members calendars.

Setting up your first calendar feed:

On left hand side above the routing, look for the gear wheel to access tour settings.

Once you click into tour settings, you will see calendar feeds towards the bottom of the page.

<img
  src="/images/general-guides/setting-up-calendar-feeds/edit-settings.png"
  alt="Main dashboard interface"
  height="300"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

Press “Add a calendar feed” to setup a new feed. From the drawer, you can create a name for the feed, and determine what information should be displayed in the calendar feed.

<img
  src="/images/general-guides/setting-up-calendar-feeds/edit-cal-feed.png"
  alt="Main dashboard interface"
  height="300"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

If you’re using groups to tag events and hotels, you can search for the “Band” or “Crew”, and setup a dedicated feed that only has the events and reservations that pertains to their group. If you want to learn more about groups and visibility, click here .

Once you’ve determined what details should be included, press save. You will now see the list of feeds populated, and you can copy the calendar feed link!

Daysheets calendar feeds update every 5 minutes, and can be used with any calendar application that accepts feeds. Congratulations! You’re now all setup with calendar feeds!
